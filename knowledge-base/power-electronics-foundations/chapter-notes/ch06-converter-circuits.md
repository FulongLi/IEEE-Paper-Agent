# Chapter 6: Converter Circuits

## Retrieval Tags

buck, boost, buck-boost, Cuk, SEPIC, isolated converters, topology comparison

## Core Ideas

- Converter topologies realize different voltage conversion functions, polarities, isolation properties, and component stress profiles.
- The choice of topology depends on input-output voltage relationship, isolation need, ripple constraints, efficiency target, and implementation complexity.
- Comparative topology analysis should include both ideal conversion behavior and practical component stress.

## Useful IEEE Language

- "The selected topology provides the required conversion ratio while maintaining acceptable device stress and passive-component size."
- "Compared with a conventional buck-boost stage, the proposed structure modifies the energy-transfer path to reduce current stress."
