# Chapter 1: Introduction

## Retrieval Tags

power electronics, switching converters, energy conversion, converter design, interdisciplinary foundations

## Core Ideas

- Power electronics studies the controlled conversion of electrical energy using semiconductor switches, passive components, magnetic components, and feedback control.
- A converter should be evaluated as an integrated system, not only as a circuit topology.
- Practical design balances efficiency, regulation, power density, thermal limits, electromagnetic interference, cost, and reliability.

## Useful IEEE Language

- "Power electronics converters provide the interface between electrical sources, storage elements, loads, and grid-connected systems."
- "The converter design problem is inherently multi-objective, coupling circuit operation, control dynamics, component stress, and implementation constraints."
