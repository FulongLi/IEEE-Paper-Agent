# Chapter 7: AC Equivalent Circuit Modeling

## Retrieval Tags

small-signal model, AC equivalent circuit, perturbation, averaged model, dynamic response

## Core Ideas

- AC equivalent circuit modeling converts switching converter dynamics into low-frequency linearized models suitable for control design.
- Small perturbations around an operating point reveal how duty-ratio, input-voltage, and load variations affect output behavior.
- The model is valid within assumptions such as small signal amplitude, fixed operating mode, and frequency range below switching harmonics.

## Useful IEEE Language

- "The small-signal model is obtained by perturbing the averaged equations around the nominal operating point."
- "The resulting AC equivalent circuit provides physical insight into the dominant dynamic elements of the converter."
