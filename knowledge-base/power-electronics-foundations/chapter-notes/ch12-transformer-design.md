# Chapter 12: Transformer Design

## Retrieval Tags

transformer, isolation, turns ratio, magnetizing inductance, leakage inductance, winding loss

## Core Ideas

- Transformer design provides voltage scaling, isolation, and energy transfer while introducing magnetizing current, leakage inductance, winding loss, and insulation constraints.
- Turns ratio, core geometry, window area, and winding arrangement jointly determine electrical stress and physical feasibility.
- Leakage inductance can be parasitic or functional, depending on topology and soft-switching strategy.

## Useful IEEE Language

- "The transformer turns ratio is selected to satisfy the required voltage conversion range while maintaining acceptable device stress."
- "Leakage inductance and winding resistance are included in the prototype loss and transient analysis."
