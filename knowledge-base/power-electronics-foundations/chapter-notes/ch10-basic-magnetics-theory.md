# Chapter 10: Basic Magnetics Theory

## Retrieval Tags

magnetic circuits, core material, flux density, magnetizing current, saturation, core loss

## Core Ideas

- Magnetic components store and transfer energy through flux linkage, core geometry, winding structure, and material properties.
- Saturation, hysteresis, eddy-current loss, and temperature dependence constrain practical inductor and transformer design.
- Magnetic design connects electrical requirements to physical size, thermal behavior, and loss distribution.

## Useful IEEE Language

- "The magnetic component is designed to avoid saturation under worst-case current stress."
- "Core loss and copper loss jointly determine the thermal performance of the magnetic structure."
