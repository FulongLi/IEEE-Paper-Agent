# Chapter 8: Converter Transfer Functions

## Retrieval Tags

control-to-output transfer function, audio susceptibility, output impedance, poles, zeros

## Core Ideas

- Converter transfer functions describe how output voltage or current responds to duty-ratio, input-voltage, and load perturbations.
- Poles and zeros determine bandwidth limits, transient behavior, and compensation requirements.
- Transfer-function interpretation links circuit parameters to control-oriented design decisions.

## Useful IEEE Language

- "The control-to-output transfer function is used to identify the dominant poles and zeros that constrain the loop bandwidth."
- "The line-to-output transfer function characterizes the converter sensitivity to input-voltage disturbances."
