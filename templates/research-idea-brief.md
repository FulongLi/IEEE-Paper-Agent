# Research Idea Brief

Use this template before asking Codex to create a paper plan.

## Raw Idea

Describe the idea in plain language.

## Domain

Example: power electronics, DC-DC converters, grid-tied inverters, digital control, magnetics, EMI.

## Target Venue

Conference, IEEE Transactions/journal, IEEE Access, or unknown.

## Problem

What problem does this work address?

## Gap

What is missing or insufficient in current approaches?

## Proposed Method

What are you proposing?

## Evidence Available

List experiments, simulations, prototypes, data, theory, or preliminary results.

## Baselines

What should the work be compared against?

## Metrics

Examples: efficiency, power density, settling time, overshoot, THD, power factor, loss, temperature rise, EMI margin.

## Constraints

Examples: page limit, hardware availability, dataset limitations, time, target application.

## Desired Output

Example: paper plan, abstract, introduction, outline, LaTeX skeleton, reviewer-style critique.
